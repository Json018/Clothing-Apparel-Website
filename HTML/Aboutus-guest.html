ABOUT US

Bihis ITee Apparel, is a start-up brand based in Bulacan, Philippines. More than just clothes and fashion, But a lifestyle that inspires and motivates. We embody the people and community in the field of Information Technology. Bihis IT aims to innovate and set trends in the industry and create a healthy and productive system that would be perceived as a testament to the dedication and sheer determination of the founders in building up the brand as a trademark in the field and serve as a status symbol for the whole IT community. In the long run, the founders aim to be able to promote Corporate Social Responsibility within the brand. "Wear our Brand, Be our Brand!"
